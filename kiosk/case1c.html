Enter Raven Sentry

In partnership with U.S. and NATO forces, Rhombus Power built Raven Sentry — an AI-powered early warning system.

It ingested vast troves of open-source data:

  • Commercial satellite imagery
  • News reports & social media
  • Messaging app chatter

With advanced machine learning, it surfaced likely attack locations — days or even weeks ahead of time.
